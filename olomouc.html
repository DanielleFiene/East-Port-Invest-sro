Mixed-Use Investment in the Heart of Olomouc

This prominent property combines ground-floor retail space with upper-floor residential apartments, offering a solid income-generating investment in a prime central location. Located in the vibrant pedestrian zone of Olomouc, the building attracts consistent foot traffic and visibility.


Retail & Residential Features

  • Ground floor retail: Currently leased to RockPoint, a national outdoor retail brand
  • Upper floors: Spacious residential apartments with modern kitchens and bathrooms
  • Historic architecture: Originally a 16th-century masonry building with Gothic cellars, later renovated in Renaissance, Baroque, and Classical styles. Currently a multi-unit residential building with commercial space, featuring a clean and functional façade adapted for modern use.
  • Separate entrances: Independent access for retail and residential tenants

Residential Comfort

The upper-floor apartments feature high-quality interiors ideal for long-term tenants or short-term rentals. Highlights include:

  • Fully equipped modern kitchens
  • Bright living rooms with city views
  • Renovated bathrooms with quality fixtures

Advantages of Investing in Olomouc

  • Stable rental market supported by universities, tourism, and growing regional demand
