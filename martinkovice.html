Martínkovice Country House

Martínkovice 254, Czech Republic

A Unique Investment Opportunity

Recently used as a rental property for large groups, this estate is perfect for hosting weddings, retreats, or exclusive events. Originally built in 1877 and renovated in 2006-2007, the total built-up area, including the courtyard, spans 1,804 m², with buildings covering approximately one-third of the plot. The beautiful courtyard features lush greenery, a covered swimming pool (10.5m x 5m, depth 1.6m), a spacious terrace, 2 indoor horse stables in the barn and an outdoor stable next to the house. Additionally, the surrounding plots total 8,217 m², ensuring both privacy and expansion opportunities.


Historical Elegance Meets Modern Comfort

The residential area of approximately 600 m² is seamlessly connected with the utility sections, including original farm structures. The property consists of 8 rooms, making it ideal for both private and commercial use. The layout includes:

Interior Layout Overview

  • 5 spacious bedrooms (or 1 second living room)
  • 6 bathrooms (1 designated as a laundry space)
  • A large living room and separate dining room
  • A well-equipped kitchen
  • A generous 60m² recreation/party room with main heating facility
  • A scenic sitting room to enjoy the picturesque surroundings

The estate features a mix of wooden and brick ceilings, enhancing its rustic charm. Across the courtyard, a large barn (26m x 11m) with a new roof serves as a garage, storage for equipment, and two horse stables. Additional buildings include a storage space (5m x 7m) and an outdoor building (10m x 8m) for events or relaxation.With minor modifications, it can be adapted for dual living, ensuring flexibility and comfort.


Prime Location in a Stunning Natural Setting

Located in one of the most beautiful natural regions of the Czech Republic, in the northeastern part of Bohemia, this estate is nestled in the picturesque village of Martínkovice. It offers a peaceful and serene environment, making it perfect for relaxation, business, or investment.


A Secure and Private Haven

The estate is enclosed by a wrought-iron gate, offering privacy and security. The climate is warmer in summer and slightly colder in winter compared to the Netherlands, providing a diverse yet comfortable living experience.


Financial Benefits of Living in the Czech Republic

  • Low income tax – 15% for the majority
  • No wealth tax
  • Low pension taxation
  • Favorable vehicle tax
  • Minimal property taxes

This remarkable estate is a rare opportunity for those seeking a spacious, historic, and versatile property in an idyllic location. Whether for personal use or as a profitable business venture, this estate offers limitless potential.
