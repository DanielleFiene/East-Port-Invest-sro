Elegant Renovated Estate with Independent Guesthouse

This beautifully restored estate blends historic Broumov regional architecture with contemporary comfort, offering an exceptional lifestyle in one of the Czech Republic’s most peaceful landscapes. Set on a generous 6,256 m² plot with a total of 800 m² usable area, the property comprises a meticulously renovated main house, an independent guesthouse, and a multifunctional third building -whether you're seeking a multigenerational family home, a countryside retreat, or a unique investment with rental potential, this property provides complete self-sufficiency, privacy, and security. Every detail — from the underfloor heating and filtered private water system to the integrated alarm network and solar panels — reflects thoughtful, high-quality craftsmanship and sustainability.


Main House Features

The main building was partially repurposed during its 2006–2008 renovation, converting its former agricultural section into a cozy living area. The ground floor includes a central hallway, living rooms, a kitchen, and bathroom facilities. A former stable now serves as a grand living hall. The upper floor features residential space with its own bathroom and a technical area. Heating is provided through underfloor systems powered by a pellet boiler.

Independent Guesthouse

The detached guesthouse is a fully functional and spacious residence in its own right. It includes several bedrooms and underfloor electric heating, a bathroom and renovated cellar, making it ideal for guest accommodation, multigenerational living, or short-term rentals — offering the same level of privacy, comfort, and high-end security as the main residence.

Additional Features

The third building, a beautifully converted barn, provides essential technical facilities and a charming winter garden. Like the main house and guesthouse, it is also secured with a comprehensive alarm system and camera surveillance, ensuring full protection across the entire estate. Its roof is equipped with photovoltaic panels (10 kW), supporting energy efficiency. Behind the garage, you’ll also find a spacious, high-ceilinged greenhouse—perfect for cultivating vegetables and herbs throughout the year. Between the main buildings, paved areas form a spacious terrace to the covered pool with built-in jet stream.
